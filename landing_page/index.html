Everything You Need. Zero Bloat.

Built on clean architecture with native Kotlin services and Flutter UI — no ads, no trackers, no server sync.

Smart Inactivity Alarm

Configurable countdown (15–120 min). When time runs out, a loud alarm with vibration triggers. Walk 100m to auto-dismiss — no snooze cheating.

Privacy First & Offline

All data lives in local SQLite. No cloud, no account, no analytics SDK. Your step counts and health data never leave your device.

Hybrid Pedometer Engine

Uses Step Detector + Step Counter sensors with an accelerometer fallback. Auto-calibrates noise floor for precise counts on any Android device.

Water Intake Tracker

Log hydration with quick +250ml/+500ml buttons. A custom wave-animated UI visualizes your progress toward a configurable daily goal.

Guided Desk Stretches

4-step timer-guided stretch routines (Neck Release, Shoulder Roll, Seated Twist, Quad Stretch) with auto-advancing 15s countdowns.

Home Screen Widgets

Pin small or large widgets showing live steps, distance, status badge, countdown timer, and an interactive pause button — no app launch needed.
